Cowboy Corn Casserole

Description

This cowboy corn casserole is a made with corn, crispy bacon, cream cheese, and Cheddar. A tasty, cheesy side dish for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • /4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 (10-ounce) packages frozen whole kernel corn
  • 10 slices bacon, crisp cooked and crumbled
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives, for garnish

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Whisk together cream cheese, milk, flour, garlic, paprika, salt and pepper in a large bowl until well combined. Stir in corn, bacon and 1 1/2 cups of the cheese. Transfer to a 2-quart baking dish. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup cheese.
  3. Bake until cheese is melted and edges are golden brown, 40 to 45 minutes.
  4. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped chives.
